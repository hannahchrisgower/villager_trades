Minecraft Villager Trades

This is a page meant to serve as a guide to essential villager trades in Minecraft. It will highlight key functions of villagers, different types of villagers and their characteristics, and the different trade patterns you can utilize in-game.

The Basics

Types of Villagers

There are 13 types of occupations that are able to be applied using a certain job block, as listed here

  • Armorer: Blast Furnace
  • Butcher: Smoker
  • Cartographer: Cartography Table
  • Cleric: Brewing Stand
  • Farmer: Composter
  • Fisherman: Barrel
  • Fletcher: Fletching Table
  • Leatherworker: Cauldron
  • Librarian: Lectern
  • Mason: Stonecutter
  • Shepherd: Loom
  • Toolsmith: Smithing Table
  • Weaponsmith: Grindstone

Villager Levels

When trading with villagers, each trade you make gives the villager a little bit of experience, and this experience gives them more trade options with each level they gain.

Villagers have 5 experience levels:

  1. Novice
  2. Apprentice
  3. Journeyman
  4. Expert
  5. Master
